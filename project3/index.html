About

This dataset comes from a survey of students in math and portuguese courses in two Italian highschools. We chose this dataset because it contains students' detailed demographic information as well as their alcohol consumption habits. We are interested in seeing whether students' demographic information affect their drinking habits, and if their alcohol consumption habits affects their academics.
